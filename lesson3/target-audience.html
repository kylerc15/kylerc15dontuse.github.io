Target Audience

The target audience will be families who are looking for things to do according to the weather. Particularly families with younger children.

Two Personas

A family of 5, two parents and 3 children.

A family of 3, one parent with two children.

Five scenarios

  • How will the weather be today?
  • What fun things can we do since the weather is good?
  • What can we do while the weather is bad?
  • What is the weather like for my friends and family in the surrounding areas?
  • Give me a storm update.
